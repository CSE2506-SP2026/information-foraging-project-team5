Discipline & Behavior Policy

Our Philosophy

We create a positive, nurturing environment where children learn to:

  • Keep themselves safe
  • Keep their friends safe
  • Keep school materials safe

Our goal is to help children develop self-control, empathy, and pride in their behavior.

How Teachers Support Positive Behavior

Teachers proactively guide behavior by:

  • Using positive reinforcement
  • Redirecting children to appropriate activities
  • Anticipating and preventing conflicts
  • Naming and validating feelings
  • Giving simple, logical, immediate consequences

Most challenges are resolved through reflection and guided problem-solving.

If Ongoing Concerns Arise

If behavior concerns continue:

  • Teachers work closely with parents
  • We collaborate to support the child's growth
  • Recommendations may be made if a different program would better meet the child's needs

Our goal is for every child to have a positive and successful school experience.
