Orientation Day

When Is Orientation?

  • Held the day after Labor Day
  • Visit length: 1 hour

What to Expect

During Orientation Day:

  • Children explore the classroom through free play
  • Families meet teachers and classmates
  • Parents receive a brief overview of the school year

First Independent School Day

  • Children attend on their own the following day
  • The class is split into two groups
  • One group attends Wednesday
  • One group attends Thursday
  • The full class attends together on Friday

This gradual start allows teachers and children to adjust in smaller groups.
