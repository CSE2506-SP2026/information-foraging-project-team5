Parent–Staff Communication

How We Share Information

Communication is ongoing throughout the year through:

  • Newsletters
  • School website updates
  • Email announcements

These provide information about classroom themes, activities, and upcoming events.

Talking With Teachers

  • Teachers may contact you by phone or email to discuss your child's adjustment, observations, or concerns.
  • If you would like to speak with a teacher or the director, please call or email the office.
  • Teachers are available by phone or email after the school day ends.

Parent Conferences & Assessment

Parent-Teacher Conferences

The school holds two conferences each year:

  • Fall: Informal meeting to get to know the child and family
  • Spring: Detailed conference with written assessment

Additional conferences may be scheduled at any time upon request by parents or teachers.

How Children Are Assessed

Assessment is ongoing and based on:

  • Classroom and outdoor observations
  • Play interactions and conversations
  • Developmental checklists and timelines
  • Samples of children's writing and artwork

Teaching teams meet weekly to discuss each child's progress and plan supportive activities.
